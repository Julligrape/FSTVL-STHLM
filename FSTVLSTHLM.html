Food and Drink

The festival area will be packed with exciting food trucks. There will also be a rich selection of refreshing alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks in our bars and at our exhibitors. Bring your friends and enjoy Sweden's hottest artists together with good food and beverages!

In addition to great live music, good food and drinks, you will be able to have fun at the festival's various activities. In true festival spirit, you and your friends will be able to play beer pong, sing karaoke or dance in our Silent Disco tent. A large Ferris wheel and fairground will also be available on the festival grounds for the thrill-seeking visitor.
